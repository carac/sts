Zenthai Shiatsu

Holistic bodywork

Zenthai Shiatsu is a modern holistic bodywork modality that draws on the traditional eastern energetic based systems of Zen Shiatsu and Traditional Thai Massage as well as the structural remediation and soft tissue techniques from western Osteopathy.

Effective therapy

By integrating the prime aspects of proven modalities and the creation of a safe, awake and caring healing environment, Zenthai Shiatsu has evolved to be widely recognised as an effective structural and energetic form of therapy.

 

Innate healing

Treatments involve massage, acupressure therapy, assisted stretching, joint mobilisation and facilitated movements that respect the bodies innate healing capacity.

Each session is designed simply to help your body reorganise itself back to its natural, free and balanced state.

Focused intention

Treatments have focused intention on restoring balance to the body systems in alignment with the Five Element Theory from Traditional Chinese Medicine.

These elements offer a diagnostic framework to help identify & correct imbalances in our structural, mental, emotional and spiritual bodies.
